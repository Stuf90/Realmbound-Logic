# Realmbound Logic — Gameplay Specification

The approved gameplay and MVP design are documented in:

- [`docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-19-realmbound-logic-mvp-design.md`](docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-19-realmbound-logic-mvp-design.md)

The two puzzle families preserve their source mechanics exactly:

- **The Royal Inquest** uses spatial Murdoku-style placement mechanics, not a category-matching logic grid.
- **Siege Lines** uses canonical Train Tracks mechanics with a medieval route-building presentation.
